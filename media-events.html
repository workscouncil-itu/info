Announcement November 2, 2025

Works Council Constitution

We are pleased to announce that the Works Council has now been officially constituted.

We are pleased to announce that the Works Council has now been officially constituted. The council elected Theodoros Saroglou as Chairperson and Julia Rippatha as Deputy Chairperson.

During the inaugural meeting, the council discussed key topics related to the upcoming program and set priorities for the next steps. In the coming period, we will establish a two-way communication system to ensure transparent and effective exchange between the council and all employees.

We look forward to constructive collaboration and to representing the interests of our community together.

Your Works Council Members:

Theodoros Saroglou, Julia Rippatha, Matthew Dold, Deler Langenberg and Michaela Lamplmayr
